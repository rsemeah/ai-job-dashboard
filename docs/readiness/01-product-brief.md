# 01. Product Brief

## Product
- **Name:** HireWire
- **Category:** AI-assisted career operating system / job application workflow tool
- **Primary promise:** Help users move from raw job listing to ready-to-apply packet (resume + cover letter + prep artifacts).

## Problem Statement
Job seekers lose momentum because research, tailoring, document writing, and interview prep are fragmented across too many tools.

## Target User (v1)
- Active individual job seeker applying to multiple roles per week.
- Has resume/history but needs faster tailoring with less guesswork.

## Why now
- High job market competition increases pressure for tailored applications.
- Users now expect AI speed but distrust generic outputs; evidence-backed outputs are a differentiator.

## Value Proposition (10-second version)
"Paste a job, get a tailored application packet and prep guidance you can trust."

## Desired User Outcome
- First value in one session: user sees role fit + draft documents for a real target job.
- Repeat value: faster weekly application throughput with higher confidence.

## Captured now
- Auth, onboarding route, job intake UI, dashboard surfaces, interview prep route, documents pages exist.
- Architecture and TruthSerum gap-audit process are documented.

## Open Questions / Missing
- Sharpen ICP by segment (early career vs. mid-career vs. career switchers).
- Define explicit non-goals for v1.
- Lock north-star metric and launch threshold.
