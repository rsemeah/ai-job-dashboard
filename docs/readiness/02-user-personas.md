# 02. User Personas

## Persona A (Primary): Active Applicant "Focused Striver"
- Applies to 5-20 roles/month.
- Main pain: tailoring each application is slow and mentally expensive.
- Wants speed without sounding generic.
- Trust barrier: fears AI hallucinations and low-quality claims.

## Persona B (Secondary): Career Switcher "Bridge Builder"
- Needs to map transferable skills to new domain language.
- Main pain: confidence gap and narrative framing.
- Trust barrier: uncertainty about what claims are defensible.

## Jobs-to-be-done by persona
1. Understand if role is worth applying to.
2. Build role-specific resume and cover letter quickly.
3. Validate quality before sending.
4. Prepare for likely interview questions.

## Current alternatives
- Manual Google Docs tailoring.
- Generic AI chats with weak persistence.
- Resume-writing services (costly, not iterative).

## Buying behavior assumptions (to validate)
- Price-sensitive, likely monthly subscription.
- Will convert after seeing quality on a real role.

## Missing / Research needed
- 8-12 user interviews with segmented notes.
- Persona-specific activation and retention benchmarks.
