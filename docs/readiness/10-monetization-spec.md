# 10. Monetization Spec

## Proposed model
- Freemium + subscription tiers.

## Free tier (proposal)
- Limited number of job analyses/month.
- Limited doc generations.
- Basic templates only.

## Paid tier (proposal)
- Higher or unlimited usage.
- Premium templates/red-team depth.
- Faster generation priority.
- Enhanced export options.

## Upgrade moments
- After first successful "aha" result.
- On hitting usage boundary.
- On attempting premium trust/quality features.

## Recovery flows
- Failed payment retry cadence.
- Grace period before downgrade.
- Transparent refund policy copy.

## Missing
- Final pricing experiments and target ARPU.
- Billing provider integration details.
- Production-ready paywall copy and eligibility rules.
